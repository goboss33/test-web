What is OpenClaw?

OpenClaw is a free and open-source autonomous AI agent that executes tasks via large language models. It uses messaging platforms as its main user interface — Telegram, Discord, WhatsApp, Signal, and more.


Think of it as your personal AI assistant that lives on your machine or server, ready to help you with coding, automation, research, and much more — all through simple chat messages.

💬
